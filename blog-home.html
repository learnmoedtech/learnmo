Innovative Techniques and Methods of E-learning

Technology has taken a predominant role in every sphere of life today.Like every other segment, the education system has also been hugely influenced by the likes of technology.There are a lot of them which have sped up the learning process and facilitated convenient learning along with it. Some of them are as follows:
E- Learning saves a considerable amount of time by eliminating the need of attending regular classes.
It is cost efficient as it terminates the cost of transport and other institutional expenses.
It enables the students to learn at their own pace and convenience.
E-learning allows the students to accommodate their studying schedule according to their learning needs.
E-learning is advantageous as it enriches the students’ knowledge in computers.
It is flexible as compared to the traditional learning methods.
It provides instant feedback on the learner’s performance. .
